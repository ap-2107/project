TRAFFIC DENSITY

The system determines the traffic density and smart timers are set in order to avoid unnecessarily long green signals and to have an efficient distribution of traffic.

STOLEN VEHICLES

Authorities will be able to find the stolen vehicles by cross-referencing the registration plates with the stored data.

FASTEST ROUTE

Emergency vehicles will be able to determine the fastest route to the required destination with the help of this system.
